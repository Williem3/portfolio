About me

This is probably your 34th website you've visited so I'll try and be brief

Java | Spring Framework | Thymeleaf | Hibernate | REST | SQL | HTML | CSS | JavaScript

Above are the technologies I have spent 6 months studying to understand. Building functional and responsive web applications have been part of my study regimen since I spent my first month learning the Java syntax and core. Utilizing Spring Boot, Hibernate, and MySQL I was able to make an Online Banking web application, Customer and Employee Manager application, and a E-commerce website.


I am a relatively new developer who set my goals to become a developer because I honestly got fed up with my initial thoughts that my brain was not wired to develop. Problem solving I am good at but when it came to code, I couldn't even imagine it. At the end of my academic career I realized that I was running away from something that was conquerable. Here I am 3 months later and I've built applications that I never would have imagined being possible.


What I'm getting at here, is that I'm someone who is willing to break past his walls and get to the meat of the problem. Doesn't matter how difficult or how much time it may take. I'm willing to go the extra mile. I look forward to seeing where I am a year from now.
